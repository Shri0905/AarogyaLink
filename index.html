About Aarogya Link

Developed by: The Rotaract Club of D.Y. Patil School of Biotechnology and Bioinformatics, Satellite City (Zone 1, RID 3142)

We are an institution-based youth organization bringing together people ages 18 and older to exchange ideas, focused on making a positive impact through community service, professional development, and cultural initiatives.

Located in Navi Mumbai, our club encourages students to initiate impactful projects in environment, education, and empowerment, while promoting teamwork, leadership, and international fellowship.

Mission: Bridging healthcare gaps through awareness, screening, and timely referrals for all communities.
